Table 1 login QR (PNG)

This page builds a scannable QR from the full signed URL (the same one your app uses for auto-login). Get that link from the app: sign in as admin → /admin/qr/table-1 → open “Show full URL (for testing)” → copy the whole line → paste below → Generate → right-click the image → Save image as… (or use Download PNG).

The QR must use the same host your guests use (match NEXTAUTH_URL). For phones on your Wi‑Fi, that is often http://YOUR_PC_IP:3000, not localhost.
